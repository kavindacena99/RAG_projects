Generative AI (GenAI) is a branch of Artificial Intelligence that can create new content such as text, images, audio, video, and code.

Unlike traditional AI systems that mainly classify or predict, Generative AI produces new outputs.

Examples:

ChatGPT generating answers
AI creating images from prompts
AI generating music
AI writing code
AI summarizing documents

Main idea:
Generative AI learns patterns from large amounts of data and then uses those patterns to create new content similar to what it learned.

Example:
If a model is trained on a huge amount of text, it can generate paragraphs, answer questions, or write emails.

Main types of Generative AI models:

Large Language Models (LLMs)
These models generate and understand text.

Examples:

GPT
Claude
Gemini
LLaMA

Uses:

Chatbots
Text generation
Summarization
Translation
Coding help
Image Generation Models
These models create images from text descriptions.

Examples:

DALL·E
Stable Diffusion
Midjourney

Uses:

Posters
Design ideas
Concept art
Marketing content
Audio and Music Generation Models
These generate speech, voice, or music.
Video Generation Models
These create or edit video content.

Important concepts in Generative AI:

Prompt: instruction given to the model
Token: small unit of text
Context Window: amount of information model can read at once
Fine-tuning: training model further for a specific task
Embeddings: numerical representations of text or data
Hallucination: when model gives false or invented information
Inference: generating output from a trained model

Popular GenAI techniques:

Transformers
Diffusion models
GANs (Generative Adversarial Networks)
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

What is RAG?
RAG combines retrieval and generation.
The system first retrieves relevant information from a database or documents, then the language model uses that information to generate a better answer.

Why RAG is useful:

Gives more accurate answers
Reduces hallucinations
Allows using private documents or knowledge bases

Future of GenAI:
Generative AI is becoming important in education, healthcare, software development, business, and research. It will likely become a major tool for productivity and creativity.

In summary, Generative AI is AI that creates new content and is transforming how people work, learn, and build software.
